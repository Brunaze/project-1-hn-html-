Meet our hosts

Colt Branson

A wandering gunslinger with a troubled past, Colt Branson is a man of few words but lethal with a revolver. Once a sheriff, now a drifter, Colt rides across the plains seeking redemption—or revenge. He offers visitors a chance to join him on dangerous bounty hunts, or simply share a quiet moment around the campfire under the stars. With his rugged charm and sense of justice, he’s a reliable companion, but beware: in Colt’s world, loyalty can be tested at any moment.

Lily Mae Harper

Lily Mae Harper is the heart and soul of her family’s ranch on the outskirts of the town. With her gentle spirit and strong work ethic, she represents the innocence of the frontier. Whether she's tending to the livestock or gathering supplies in town, Lily Mae offers visitors a glimpse into the simpler, yet often challenging life on the homestead. However, beneath her kind exterior, she hides a deep desire for adventure, waiting for the right guest to take her on a journey beyond the confines of the farm.

Jasper "Red Jack" Hayes

Feared across the West, Jasper "Red Jack" Hayes is an infamous outlaw with a price on his head and a trail of chaos behind him. Known for his sharp wit and quicker trigger, Red Jack is always on the run from bounty hunters and lawmen. Visitors can either choose to join him on daring robberies or try their luck at bringing him to justice. Charismatic yet ruthless, Red Jack always has a trick up his sleeve, making him one of the most dangerous and unpredictable hosts in the park.
